About Me

Hi, I’m Sivraj, a research scholar in Structural Engineering at IIT Delhi. My work focuses on the design and detailing of steel-concrete composite beam-column joints, especially CFST columns connected to steel beams using external diaphragms, combining experimental and numerical studies.

I completed my M. Tech. in Structural Dynamics and Earthquake Engineering from VNIT Nagpur (2016–2018). Before joining my Ph.D. in 2021, I worked as a Senior Engineer at Thornton Tomasetti Mumbai, designing steel connections as per AISC standards, and as a Trainee Design Engineer at STUP Consultants, working on bridge design and analysis.

Research Interests

  • Steel-concrete composite structures
  • Beam-column joints
  • Structural detailing and design
  • Seismic performance of composite frames
