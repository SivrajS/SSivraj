About Me

I am a research scholar at IIT Delhi working on the design and detailing of steel-concrete composite beam column joints. My research focuses on improving structural performance, connection detailing, and seismic resilience of composite frames.

Research Interests

  • Steel-concrete composite structures
  • Beam-column joints
  • Structural detailing and design
  • Seismic performance of composite frames
